What’s greening out?

Greening out is the unpleasant experience after consuming too much cannabis, leading to symptoms such as nausea, dizziness, anxiety, and light-headedness. It can happen to both new and experienced users. Feeling overwhelmed or uncomfortable is common during such episodes. For more details, click here.
