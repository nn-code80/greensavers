What are GreenSavers products?

Green Savers power mix is designed to counteract the discomforts of greening out. Crafted with a unique blend of highly-concentrated glucose, they offer relief from symptoms like nausea and dizziness by boosting blood sugar levels, making them helpful during recovery.
